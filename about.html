About the Author

Meet Makalakshmi S

Hello! I'm Makalakshmi S, a first-year Computer Science and Engineering student with a passion for both technology and storytelling. My journey combines the logical world of programming with the creative realm of fiction writing.

As a computer science student, I've developed strong skills in C++, Python, C, HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. My problem-solving abilities help me craft intricate plots and complex characters that keep readers engaged from the first page to the last.

My writing explores the complexities of human nature across multiple genres—from literary fiction to psychological thrillers. Each book I write is an exploration of the questions that keep us awake at night and the truths we're afraid to confront.

When I'm not coding or writing, you can find me reading voraciously, learning new programming languages, and working on exciting tech projects that blend creativity with innovation.

My Writing Philosophy

"I write to understand the world and my place in it. Every character I create is a mirror reflecting some aspect of humanity—the beautiful, the broken, and everything in between. My goal is not to provide answers, but to ask the right questions and invite readers on a journey of discovery."
